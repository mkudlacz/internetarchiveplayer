                        Settings

                        Collection ID

                        Enter the collection identifier from archive.org/details/identifier. Any public audio collection on the Internet Archive works — tapers, radio archives, live music vaults, and more. The app title will update to match the collection name.

                        Favorites Backup

                        This app stores all data locally. To backup or to migrate your favorites to another browser, use the "Export Favorites Link" above and copy the provided URL.

                        Restore Favorites

                        Paste your exported link above and tap Restore.

                        Time Travel
                        Tap any Discover card to load a full multi-artist night into your queue.
                        Today · Popular
                        Shows from today across all years, or the top plays. Tap ↺ to reshuffle.
                        Play a show
                        Tap Play All or any track. Long-press a track for Play Next or Add to Queue.
                        Queue
