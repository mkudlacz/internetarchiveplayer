Favs
All your saved shows, organized by artist. Tap the ♥ on any show, card, or list item to add it.
Search
The search bar filters whichever tab you're on — artists, venues, dates, or show titles. Tap the ✕ or press Escape to clear.
Playing a Show
  • 1
    Tap any show in a list or card to open its detail page.
  • 2
    Tap Play All to load the whole concert, or tap any track in the list to start from that point. Either one opens the Now Playing bar at the bottom.
  • 3
    Add to Queue appends all the tracks without interrupting what's playing.
  • 4
    Long-press any track for more options: Play Next, Add to Queue, or Go to Artist.
  • 5
    Tap the album art thumbnail on the detail page to open a full-screen lightbox with a higher-resolution image.

Queue
  • 1
    Tap the Queue button (≡) in the Now Playing bar to open it.
  • 2
    Drag any track up or down to reorder it.
  • 3
    Long-press any queue item for options: Go to Show, Go to Artist, or Delete from the queue.
  • 4
    Tap Clear in the top-left to remove everything and start fresh.
Favorites
Saving a show
Tap the ♥ on any show detail page, list item, or Discover card. The heart turns amber. Tap again to unsave.
Backup & restore
All favorites are stored locally on your device. To back up or move them to another browser, go to Settings → Export Favorites Link and copy the URL. Paste it into Settings → Restore on any device to import.
Tips
Time Travel to a Show — tap any card in this Discover section to instantly load a complete multi-artist night into your queue. Great for discovering acts you'd never search for directly.
Today in the Archive — every show in the collection recorded on today's date, across all years. Scroll through to see who played on this date in 1989, 1994, 2003…
Popular — the most-played shows in the collection. Results are reshuffled each visit. Tap the ↺ button to reshuffle again without reloading.
Concert detail — each show page includes an "On This Date" note with the weather that night and a Chicago historical event from the same date, when available.
Search is tab-aware — searching on the Artists tab finds artists, searching on Venues finds venues. Switch tabs to change what you're filtering.
Install as an App
Add to Home Screen
Open the app in Safari on iPhone, tap the Share button, then tap Add to Home Screen. This gives you a full-screen experience with no browser chrome, lock-screen controls, and the app icon on your home screen.
