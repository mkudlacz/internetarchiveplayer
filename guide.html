Playing a Show
  • 1
    Tap any show in a list or card to open its detail page.
  • 2
    Tap Play All to load the whole concert, or tap any track to start from that point. Either opens the Now Playing bar.
  • 3
    Add to Queue appends all tracks without interrupting what's currently playing.
  • 4
    Long-press any track for more options: Play Next, Add to Queue, or Go to Artist.
  • 5
    Tap the album art thumbnail to open a full-screen lightbox with a higher-resolution image.

Queue & Favorites
Using the Queue
Tap ≡ in the Now Playing bar to open it. Long-press any item to go to its show, go to its artist, or remove it. Tap Clear to wipe everything.
Saving shows
Tap ♥ on any show detail page, list item, or Discover card. The heart turns amber. Tap again to unsave. Find everything in the Favs tab.
Backup & restore
All favorites are stored locally on your device. To move them to another browser, go to Settings → Export Favorites Link and copy the URL. Paste it into Settings on any device to restore.
Tips & Tricks
Time Travel — tap any card in the Discover section to instantly load a complete multi-artist night into your queue.
Today in the Archive — see who played on this date in 1989, 1994, 2003… across all years in the collection.
Popular — the most-played shows. Tap ↺ to reshuffle without reloading the page.
Concert detail — shows the weather that night, a Chicago history note from that date, and total play count.
Search is tab-aware — searching on Artists finds artists, Venues finds venues. Switch tabs to change what you're filtering.
Browse any collection — works with any public Internet Archive audio collection. Open Settings, enter a collection ID, tap Save & Reload.
Settings & Install
Switch collections
Open Settings (gear icon), enter any Internet Archive audio collection ID — the part after archive.org/details/ — and tap Save & Reload. The app title updates automatically.
Add to Home Screen
In Safari on iPhone, tap the Share button, then Add to Home Screen. Full-screen experience, lock-screen controls, app icon on your home screen.
